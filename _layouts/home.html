Survival Swim Lessons: 6 months and older

20 minutes @ $23.00

1:1 survival swim lesson focused on getting baby/child safe in the water. Kids will work on breath control, going under water, back floating in a variety of different scenarios, and exiting the pool safely. We aim to achieve a 60 second unassisted back float. Once the safety skills are acquired, we will move onto stroke work.

We focus on the 6 main strokes, front crawl (freestyle), back crawl (back stroke), side stroke, elementary back stroke, butterfly, breast stroke. There will also be some dive work.

***Lessons can begin at 6 months+ if developmentally appropriate.
