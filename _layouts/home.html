How to Book

  • Plan

    Our easy-to-use scheduling system lets you pick the days and times that work for your family's busy life. Lessons are booked one month at a time, with no extra fees for reschedules or cancellations*. The next month's schedule goes live two weeks before lessons start - typically on Mondays at 7 pm. For updates, follow us on Instagram @gemswimidaho.

    *Please see our cancellation policy for full details.

  • Sign Up

    For your first time swimming with us, you need to pick 'First Time Lesson with Gem Swim.' It'll give you a form to fill out, our policies, and what to expect before you join us. After your initial lesson, go ahead and choose the 'Survival/Stroke Lesson.' If you've got multiple kids, select as many lesson slots as needed for each of them. All our lessons are one-on-one with an instructor, designed to suit your child's needs for that day.

  • Find Lessons

    After you've picked the type of lesson, you can choose a specific instructor or simply go with the 'Any Available' option. It'll show you the open lesson slots. Just select your preferred date and time, specify the number of lessons you want, and then book with an instructor.

    Here's an important tip! When you choose a lesson time, you'll see options like 'select and continue', 'select and add another time', or 'select and make it recurring'. This lets you book multiple times during the month in one go, saving you time.
